Fiction Recommendations

  • “Whispers in the Mist” - In a secluded coastal town, secrets buried for generations resurface when a young journalist investigates a series of eerie occurrences. As the mist thickens, so do the whispers of long-lost tragedies.
  • “Echoes of Eternity” - Against the backdrop of ancient Rome, a slave girl and a senator’s son fall in love—a forbidden passion that echoes through time. Their fates intertwine as they navigate treacherous politics, gladiator arenas, and the whispers of gods.

Non-Fiction Recommendations

  • "How to Think Like a Roman Emperor" by Donald Robertson: Presents Marcus Aurelius’s beliefs as a self-help guide.
  • "The Epic Siege of World War II, 1941-1944" by Anna Reid: Chronicles life in Leningrad during a significant military blockade
